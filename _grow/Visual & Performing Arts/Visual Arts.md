<h3>OBJECTIVES</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To encourage and develop the reinforce stronger culture of Arts in Westwood</p>

Art Making and Art Appreciation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To build confidence in self-expression through art</p>
</li>
