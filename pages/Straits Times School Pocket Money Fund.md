<p>We are pleased to inform you that the application period for The Straits
Times School Pocket Money Fund for students from low-income families will
be open from <strong>20 January 2026 to 13 February 2026</strong>.</p>
<p>Submit the <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1CDU_eP0NXKDaJdhzTevteT6lwVzsDAsV/view?usp=sharing" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Application Form</a> with
the necessary complete supporting documents to the General Office (“GO”)
by <strong>Friday</strong>  <strong>13 February 2026</strong>. Late submission
will not be entertained.</p>
<p>Thank you for your co-operation and understanding.</p>
