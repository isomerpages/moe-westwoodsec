<h3>VISION/GOALS</h3>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>A culture to innovate and initiate for the community</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To provide an inclusive and integrated environment that shapes independent
and creative individuals who contribute towards the community</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Values</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Resilience &amp; Integrity - Do the right thing even when the going gets
tough</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Innovation, Compassion, &amp; Continual Learning - Seek opportunities
to serve and grow in all endeavors</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Goals</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To equip pupils with necessary knowledge, skills, and attitudes so that
they are competent in their coursework</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To provide platforms for innovation to take place</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To provide a synergy of curriculum packages to equip students with skills
to adapt to changes in the future</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p>To provide students with opportunities to conduct research using various

reports, posters, videos or show and tell presentation in class. Through
this project, students get to treasure our heritage and take pride in shaping
our own unique way of life in Singapore. Based on their research conducted,
students will create and prepare a fusion dish for practical exam in Term
