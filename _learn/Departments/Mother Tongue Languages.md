<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Festival Celebrations</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Term 1 to 4</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>The festival Celebrations are vibrant and diverse, reflecting the multicultural
tapestry of the nation. These celebrations include Chinese New Year, Deepavali,
Hari Raya Puasa, and Mid Autum Festival, etc. Students eagerly participate
in various activities such as traditional dances, music performances, arts
and crafts workshops, and food fairs, showcasing the richness of each cultural
tradition. These festivals not only foster a sense of pride and belonging
among students of different backgrounds but also promote cross-cultural
understanding and appreciation within the school community. Through these
festivities, our students celebrate diversity, unity, and the spirit of
inclusivity.</p>
</td>
</tr>

</td>

<p>Term 1 to 2</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>The objective of MTL Fortnight is to ignite students’ interest and appreciation
for the Mother Tongue Languages and culture. It provides an enhanced and
immersive learning experience for students through various cultural workshops
and engaging language activities within and beyond the classroom.</p>
</td>
