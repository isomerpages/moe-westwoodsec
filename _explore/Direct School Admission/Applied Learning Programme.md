<p><strong>About</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Westwood’s Applied Learning Programme (ALP) in Innovation and Social Entrepreneurship
(ISE) develops students into innovative and enterprising individuals who
are ready to tackle real-world challenges.</p>
<p>Through a design thinking approach, students learn to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Empathise with users</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Identify meaningful problems</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop and test solutions</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Students build key 21st Century Competencies (21CC) such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Adaptive Thinking</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Communication</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaboration</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Learning goes beyond the classroom through authentic experiences, allowing
students to apply their learning in meaningful ways.</p>
